## Route based Tabs example

```jsx
<Tabs>
  <Tab
    title="Essentials"
    path="product/22"
    component={<EssentialsTab product={product} />}
  />
  <Tab
    title="Inventory Flow"
    path="product/22/inventory"
    component={<Segment>...</Segment>}
  />
  <Tab
    title="Analytics"
    path="product/22/analytics"
    component={<Segment>Charts and stuff</Segment>}
  />
  <Tab
    title="Website"
    path={Urls.productWebsitePath(product.id)}
    component={<Segment>Charts and stuff</Segment>}
  />
</Tabs>
```
